About us

Founded in 2023, our boutique was created by five friends — Sarbalina Assylzada, Kusayyin Sabina, Dzhumahmetov Amir, Nurasbek Alikhan, and Kumarov Yerzhas — who share one passion: bringing timeless elegance to every woman.

Sarbalina Assylzada, our Creative Director, leads the vision and design of each collection. Kusayyin Sabina manages branding and marketing, Dzhumahmetov Amir handles web and technology, Nurasbek Alikhan oversees logistics, and Kumarov Yerzhas directs finance and strategy.

We curate a hand-picked selection of designer pieces from Christian Louboutin, Dior, Yves Saint Laurent, Jimmy Choo, and Prada, along with exclusive collaborations with Elie Saab. Our online boutique offers elegant gowns, handbags, and shoes — each chosen to inspire confidence and beauty. Every order is handled with care, because for us, fashion is more than style — it's a feeling of grace, confidence, and belonging.
